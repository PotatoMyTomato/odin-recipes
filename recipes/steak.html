Peppercorn Steak

Description

The best pairing to any seafood starter is always going to be steak. Surf n' turf is a popular combination for a reason. What better way than to pair oysters seasoned with peppercorn than peppercorn steak? The answer is nothing is better!

This steak is seasoned to sizzling perfection through the use of peppercorn and allows a sensational taste to a sensational evening for your guests!
