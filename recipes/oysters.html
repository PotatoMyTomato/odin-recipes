Rockin' Oysters Rockefeller

Description

This delectable recipe was created in 1899 and named after John D. Rockefeller due to the richness of the sauce that marinates the dish. It is best known to be served as an appetizer with a cold refreshing beer.

Making this dish the starter of a three course meal is sure to be a major hit in every way possible with any of your guests. This sort of recipe is not used often as oysters have a particular repuation attached to them, however, this recipe is sure to please even the apprehensive. When someone says they hate oysters, chances are they haven't tried the Rockin' Oysters Rockefeller
